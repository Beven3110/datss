Policy Pack

The Policy Pack contains essential guidelines and procedures that must be understood before commencing work at DATSS. Each policy is designed to ensure safe, compliant, and high-quality service delivery. After reviewing each policy, you will be required to complete a short quiz and achieve a satisfactory score (out of 10) to demonstrate your understanding.

Application Pack

The Application Pack is a critical step in the onboarding process. It includes all required documentation such as personal details, certifications, and background checks. Once completed, you will be able to upload your documents through the provided submission option for review and approval.

Induction Pack

The Induction Pack provides the training and key information required to begin working safely and effectively at DATSS. It covers your responsibilities, workplace expectations, and essential procedures to ensure you are fully prepared for your role.
